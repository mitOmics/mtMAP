MTMap — Mitochondrial Genome Mapper Pyodide

Upload up to 30 mitochondrial genomes in GenBank format (.gb/.gbk/.gbff) and generate the linearized genome map, the CDS/rRNA presence heatmap, and the normalized-position distance heatmap. Downloads in PNG, SVG, and CSV (plus a ZIP with everything).

Tip: use the built-in self-tests below to validate parsing in your browser before uploading large files.

Built-in self-tests

Minimal GenBank fixtures to exercise the JS→Pyodide byte conversion and Biopython parsing.
